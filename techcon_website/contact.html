About Me

I am a dedicated and innovative chemical engineer with extensive experience in standardization and telecommunications. My career began with an internship at Famdel Industries Ltd in Accra, where I developed my skills in computer networking. I continued to build my expertise as a trainee at the Admin Telecom Academy, where I received training in microwave link surveying, installation, and fiber optic testing. My professional journey has taken me to the Ghana Standards Authority, where I have been instrumental in developing and revising standards for telecommunications and ICT systems.
